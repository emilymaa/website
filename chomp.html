The Project

E2BM is a two-sided platform that allows businesses to rent small spaces in existing stores to sell their products. This allows small businesses to book spaces for flexible amounts of time to increase their physical presence without incurring fixed rental payments and existing businesses can reduce their rent expense.

My Contribution

I helped to conduct user interviews, create wireframes, designed all visual assets and the final prototype.

the problem:

During the pandemic, small businesses have been impacted drastically. With all the new restrictions and lockdown in place, small business owners have been having to pay an excessive amount of rent for their commercial space, even though it's not being used.

At the same time, the pandemic has sparked many thriving new e-commerce businesses. According to the Census Bureau, 4.4 million new businesses have been created in the US since last march.



"How can we bridge the gap in a post pandemic world with e-commerce businesses who want to continue their growth, while brick and mortar businesses look to make up some of the lost revenues?"
