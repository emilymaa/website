Description

Hack the 6ix is the largest summer student run, not for profit hackathon located in the heart of Toronto. Every year, we select 400+ participants from a pool of over 1500 remarkable developers, designers, and innovators.

My Role

As a marketing director, I am in charge of developing an integrated marketing plan by creating social media campaigns, email marketing, blogs, and forming cross organizational partnerships.
